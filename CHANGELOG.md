## 📜 Changelog

### [1.1.4] - 2025-03-01
#### Added
- Initial release with core inventory management features
- Dashboard with key metrics and recent activities
- Product and variant management
- Stock item tracking per warehouse
- Purchase order creation and receiving
- Stock movement logging
- Low stock and out‑of‑stock reporting
- Comprehensive audit logging
- User authentication and role‑based access
- Settings page with configurable options
- Export to CSV, Excel, and PDF for all lists and reports
- Sales report with monthly trends and top products
- Profit & loss report with expense breakdown
- Inventory report with category distribution and supplier performance
